Eid Al Adhd Donation

The Prophet (ﷺ) said, “No good deeds done on other days are superior to those done on these (first ten days of Dhul Hijjah).”

As we’re approaching Dhul Hijjah, make the first days of the “Month of the Pilgrimage” count. Even if you can’t fulfill your Hajj duty, there’re many ways to reap the immense blessings of this sacred month.

Donating to those in need is your direct pathway to gain great rewards.

At Africa Relief, we channel your generous donations to empower vulnerable communities across Africa. It’s the best time of the year to support our projects – Water, Food Aid, Health Initiative, Orphan Sponsorships, Education.

Commemorate the Day of Arafah

You don’t only earn your protection from punishment this month. You can also celebrate the day Allah (SWT) perfected his religion, completed His favors upon his beloved Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), and approved Islam as a way of life—the Day of Arafah. Make the most of it by helping those in need have a decent life.
