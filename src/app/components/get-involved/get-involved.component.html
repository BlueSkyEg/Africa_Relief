Partnership Grant

Partner your company/organization with Africa Relief today to start making a difference in the lives of many!

The Partnership Grant Application Africa Relief and Community Development (ARCD) was established to address the immediate and long-term needs of poverty-stricken communities in Africa by providing comprehensive relief and developmental aid.

Criteria for Grant Requests: ARCD will consider grant requests to organizations that:

  • Are non-profit entities
  • Support programs related to water and sanitation, orphan sponsorship, student sponsorship, food aid, and/or healthcare aid.
  • Are working within West, South, East, and/or Central Africa
  • Have been in operation for a minimum of 5 consecutive years, and can provide a portfolio containing all completed and in-progress reports when submitting the application
