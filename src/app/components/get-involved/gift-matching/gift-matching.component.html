Amplify Your Impact With Matching Gifts

Your donation could go even further with your employer’s corporate gift matching program. Many companies offer matching gift programs to support their employees’ charitable contributions, doubling—or even tripling—their impact. 
That means more food for families, expanded access to clean drinking water, more orphaned children cared for, and more medical supplies sent to clinics all over the world . 

Enrolling in a matching gifts program takes only 3 steps:

Verify that your employer participates in a matching gift program.

Many companies offer matching gift programs to support their employees’ charitable contributions

Confirm that ARCD is eligible under your employer’s matching gift program

Once you’ve determined that your employer will match your contribution to ARCD,

  • Consult with your Human Resources team to obtain any necessary forms you’ll need to fill out.
  • Send your completed form—with a copy of your donation receipt—to ARCD’s headquarter office: Attn: Matching Gifts, 65 Kingsland Ave. Suite #2 Clifton, NJ 07014. 
  • If your employer uses an online matching gift portal, complete the online form, noting the our address listed above. ARCD’s Tax Identification No. is 46-2568671.
